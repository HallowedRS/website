Bot Documentation

Hallowed RS Utilities is a bespoke Discord bot developed exclusively for the Old School RuneScape Clan, Hallowed RS. It offers a set of tailored tools designed to streamline and enhance the management of clan-specific functionalities. This bot is meticulously crafted for the Hallowed RS clan's needs and is not designed for broad usage.

Although Hallowed RS Utilities is a proprietary bot and only Senior Staff have access to its source code, we deeply appreciate and value community input. If you possess ideas for enhancements or innovative features, please reach out directly to @connorfield via Discord or GitHub.

Hallowed RS Utilities is a protected project, exclusive to Hallowed RS. All rights reserved.

List Points

Allows any clan member to view their points, or the points of another member.

Add Points

Empowers our staff members to credit Piety points to a clan member.

Remove Points

Enables Senior Staff to subtract Piety points from any member.
